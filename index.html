  • Implimented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting commucational devices. Automated attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the invitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payrell data.
    2. Economics Have you ever thought attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting and end to inaccurate time recording, buddy punching, absenteeium, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Assium Group found that large organisations save up to $1,600 per employee afer implimenting automated attendance management systems.
